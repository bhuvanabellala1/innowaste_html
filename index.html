What we do

InnoWaste offers small medium businesses a socially equitable and cost effective recycling collection service that specifically aims to improve the livelihood of lower income recyclers.

Problem

Every day, hundreds of thousands of people scavenge all over the US collecting recyclable materials that they can sell for cash. This cash is one of their primary sources of income. These informal recyclers work in unsafe conditions and are cast in a negative light by society, at large. On the other hand, small and medium businesses pay large amounts of money to corporate waste haulers to get rid of these same recyclables. The services that these businesses use are very expensive, have inflexible schedules, and lack transparency.

Solution

We will address the needs of these two symbiotic parties by connecting them. Through Innowaste, small and medium businesses can schedule pickups at their discretion for a fixed price per pickup. Innowaste will provide recycling bins and other such physical capital to these businesses. On the other side, any individual can sign up to become an Innowaste hauler. On the app, our Innowaste haulers can choose to pickup recycling materials that is the most convenient to them. Our haulers will be paid a fixed amount per pickup. The haulers are required to take these materials to a recycling center. We highly encourage our haulers to take these recyclable materials to a buy-back center to make additional income.

Platform

Our mobile application is a two-sided platform designed to provide a seamless, hassle-free experience to both SMBs and haulers. SMBs can choose the type and quantity of recyclables they want to be picked. They can then pick a time frame for the pickup with an option of making these pickups recurrent. Our haulers will then be shown all the available pickups based on their mode of transportation and location. Once the hauler has successfully made a pickup, they then will be guided to the nearest recycling center.

Impact

Currently, businesses pay a lot of money for an inflexible service. Sometimes these businesses are not even aware of what they are paying for. By signing up with us, businesses can save upwards of 100% on their recycling bill, know exactly what they are paying for, and meet their CSR requirements. Our haulers, on the other hand, can supplement their income by making as much as $450/week through pick up fees from 8-10 pickups and the sale of recyclables. At Innowaste, our goal is to save money for the businesses and supplement income for the underemployed by redistributing the money paid to corporate haulers.
